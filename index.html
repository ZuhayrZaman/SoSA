The Software Students Association SoSA ry, commonly known as SoSA, is the official guild for software engineering students at LUT University’s Lahti campus. We’re all about creating a vibrant, supportive, and slightly chaotic (in the best way) community. SoSA organizes events that help you connect, decompress, and thrive. We also provide access to facilities because every software engineer deserves a space to build, break, and rebuild. If you're a newcomer we get it, stepping into university life can feel a bit intense. But don’t panic! We’ve compiled a Fresher Guide that covers everything from surviving your first week to finding the best coffee on campus. It’s your cheat sheet to Lahti life, student culture, and all the little things that make this guild feel like home. [Want to Join us ? →](https://www.sosa.fi/freshers)
